Mission Zero Emission

Welcome to Mission Zero Emission, an innovative platform designed to revolutionize how we measure and reduce the energy consumption of Java programs. Our app leverages JoularJX, a powerful energy measurement tool, to monitor the energy usage of Java and JAR files. By combining this data with real-time carbon intensity information from electricity grids, we provide detailed insights into the environmental impact of software development.

Key Features:

  • Energy Measurement: Accurately measure the energy consumed by your Java programs.
  • Carbon Footprint Analysis: Calculate the carbon footprint based on energy consumption and regional carbon intensity.
  • Data Visualization: Understand your impact with clear, intuitive graphs and charts.
  • Interactive Analysis: Experiment with runtime complexities to explore how program design influences energy efficiency.

Our Mission

At Mission Zero Emission, our goal is to empower developers to make sustainable choices, fostering a future where technology and the environment coexist harmoniously. By providing actionable insights, we aim to bridge the gap between software performance and ecological responsibility. Join us on this journey toward energy-efficient and eco-friendly programming!
